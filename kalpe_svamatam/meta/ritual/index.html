Language

Ritual (mantra-s and hymns therein) can involve specialized language. The use and preservation of sanskrit for ritual purposes was an important ancestral IE trait - seen in Lithuanians, Romans and Indo-Aryans (ref. patanjalI).

Yet, local languages were harmoniously integrated in later ritual systems which involved non IE devotees. Though, some opposers of Agama and veda-s have tried to use this as a dividing point (Chidambaram08 HK).

Initiation

A western pagan reconstructionist’s view (KR16) with divination for compatibility testing etc.. is consonant with many hindu mantra traditions.

Eligibility limitations

  • The right to exclusivity (based on gender, stage of life, varNa, position etc..) naturally applies.
